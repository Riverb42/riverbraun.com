About

A content strategist who actually writes — and a writer who understands what makes the phone ring.

Most travel writers can tell a beautiful story but have no idea how to get it in front of the people who need to read it. And most SEO people can dissect a dashboard but write copy you'd never read voluntarily. I sit in the middle of that Venn diagram — and it turns out that's a pretty valuable place to be.

I spent years building content systems in one of the most competitive industries around, learning what actually moves the needle when the goal isn't just "nice article" but "booked room." Keyword architecture that captures real intent. Editorial calendars that build on themselves over time. Automation workflows that keep content flowing without anyone losing sleep over next week's blog post.

Now I bring that systems-first thinking to travel and hospitality — an industry full of extraordinary stories that too often go untold, because the people who know the destinations best aren't always the ones who know how to get those stories in front of the right traveler at the right moment.

I'm based in California. I visit the places I write about. The content comes from the ground — real observations, real details, real conversations with the people who make a place what it is.
